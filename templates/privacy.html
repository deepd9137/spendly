Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is processed within 30 days of your request.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This means your browser may make a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No financial or account data is shared with Google or any other third party as part of normal app operation.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We take privacy concerns seriously and will respond promptly.
